Rule 12 - Pet a cat when you encounter one on the street

As per my understanding

Life includes suffering we can't negotiate away. This rule reminds me to notice the small, bright coins: sunlight on the desk, tea that's exactly right, a cat that decides I'm worthy today.

Beauty isn't a luxury, it's fuel for carrying the load. Pausing for wonder doesn't deny pain, it keeps me strong enough to keep going.

Key takeaways

  • Notice small, beautiful moments, they refuel resilience.
  • Joy doesn't cancel pain, it equips me to carry it.
  • Practice deliberate attention: tiny gratitude, daily.
