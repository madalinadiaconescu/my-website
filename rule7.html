Rule 7 - Pursue what is meaningful, not what is expedient

As per my understanding

Shortcuts are loud, meaning is quiet and heavy. I'm learning to choose the uncomfortable yes, the project that teaches me, the friendship that asks for honesty, the workout I don't want to do. Meaning costs time and ego, but it pays in dignity. When I align my actions with what I value, momentum shows up like a friend who's always on time.

Key takeaways

  • Trade short comfort for long significance.
  • Meaning grows from responsibility taken on purpose.
  • Schedule the hard thing first, let values set the agenda.
