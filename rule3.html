Rule 3 - Make friends with people who want the best for you

As per my understanding

I don't need an audience, I need a crew. The people closest to me either help me climb or quietly cheer for my fall. I'm choosing friends who celebrate discipline, curiosity, and small wins and who will call me out with kindness. Loyalty isn't covering each other's bad decisions, it's steering each other back to the path. My future self deserves relationships that build, not drain.

Key takeaways

  • Audit my circle: choose builders over spectators.
  • Good friends celebrate wins and challenge excuses.
  • Loyalty means honesty plus support, not mutual sabotage.
