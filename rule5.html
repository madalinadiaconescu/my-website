Rule 5 — Do not let your children do anything that makes you dislike them

As per my understanding

I don't have kids, but this rule still made an impact. Chaos today becomes cost tomorrow. Good boundaries are acts of love: they protect what matters and teach consequences early, when the stakes are small. If I ever lead siblings, teammates, or future kids, my job isn't to be liked every minute, it's to shape behavior I can respect.

Key takeaways

  • Love needs boundaries; boundaries prevent resentment.
  • Correct early, calmly, and consistently—keep the stakes small.
  • Apply it to myself: discipline now protects future me.
