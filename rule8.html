Rule 8 - Tell the truth, or at least don’t lie

As per my understanding

Lying fractures my map of reality. Every small untruth creates a version of me that has to keep acting. Telling the truth is less about moral points and more about staying integrated: one story, one person. If I can't say the full truth, I can say, “I'm not ready to answer.” Clarity is hard in the moment and liberating afterward.

Key takeaways

  • Small lies fracture self-respect and relationships.
  • Clarity is kind; “I don't know yet” is honest.
  • One story, one self—integrity simplifies life.
