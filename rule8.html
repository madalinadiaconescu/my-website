Rule 8 - Tell the truth, or at least don’t lie

As per my understanding

Lying fractures my map of reality. Every small untruth creates a version of me that has to keep acting. The cost is confusion and distance from others and from myself.

Telling the truth isn't about moral points, it's about staying integrated. If I'm not ready to answer, I can say that. Clarity is hard in the moment and liberating afterward.

Key takeaways

  • Small lies fracture self-respect and relationships.
  • Clarity is kind; “I don't know yet” is honest.
  • One story, one self—integrity simplifies life.
