Rule 2 - Treat yourself like someone you are responsible for helping

As per my understanding

I'm strangely better at caring for others than for myself. This rule asks me to be my own advocate: to rest, to eat properly, to study with focus, to tell myself the truth. If I were responsible for a friend, I'd make plans and follow through, so I should do that for me, too. Self-respect isn't indulgence, it's maintenance. I'm not a project to abandon, I'm a person I'm accountable for.

Key takeaways

  • Be your own advocate: plan rest, food, study, and boundaries.
  • Self-care is maintenance, not indulgence.
  • Talk to yourself like you would to someone you love and coach.
