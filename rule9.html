Rule 9 - Assume the person you’re listening to might know something you don’t

As per my understanding

Listening is a power move. When I suspend the need to win the conversation, I collect data I didn't know I needed. People become generous when they feel understood, solutions appear that arguments never find. Curiosity beats cleverness. I can still disagree later, but I'll do it with a better model of the world.

Key takeaways

  • Listen to learn, not to reload; mirror, ask, pause.
  • Seek disconfirming evidence, it upgrades my map.
  • Real understanding beats winning the moment.
