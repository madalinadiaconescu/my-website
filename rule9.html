Rule 9 - Assume the person you’re listening to might know something you don’t

As per my understanding

Listening is a power move. When I pause the urge to win the conversation, I collect data I didn't know I needed. People get generous when they feel understood.

I can disagree later after I've upgraded my map. Curiosity beats cleverness, questions beat speeches.

Key takeaways

  • Listen to learn, not to reload; mirror, ask, pause.
  • Seek disconfirming evidence, it upgrades my map.
  • Real understanding beats winning the moment.
