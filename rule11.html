Rule 11 - Do not bother children when they are skateboarding

As per my understanding

Growth needs real risk. Wrapped in bubble wrap, we stay small. I need challenges that can bruise my ego and sharpen my judgment.

I'm letting myself try hard things and letting others do the same. Courage is built by contact with reality, not commentary from the sidelines.

Key takeaways

  • Growth needs real risk, overprotection breeds fragility.
  • Let myself (and others) try hard things and learn from falls.
  • Courage is built by contact with reality.
